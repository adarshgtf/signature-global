About Infinity Mall

The Pursuit Of Perfection

Infinity is more than just a destination—it’s an experience. Spread across 2.15 acres, Infinity redefines urban living with state-of-the-art architecture, luxurious interiors, and a curated mix of the finest retail brands, multiplexes, food courts, entertainment zones, restaurants, and a terrace café.

Designed as a one-of-its-kind landmark in the neighbourhood, Infinity is an oasis of shopping and recreation for over 5 lakh residents in the vicinity, as well as a refreshing stopover for travellers, thanks to its excellent connectivity to NH-8 and the Delhi–Mumbai Industrial Corridor. Here, every corner inspires indulgence and discovery. From the essentials you seek to the experiences you never knew you needed, Infinity connects you to countless ways of leisure, lifestyle, and luxury.

Know More About

Signature Global is a leading real estate developer in Delhi NCR, reshaping the region’s housing landscape with a strong presence in the mid and premium housing segments. Established in 2014, the company has rapidly grown to hold a 13% market share in NCR and 27% in Gurugram’s micro-markets. Backed by reputed investors like Nomura, HDFC, IFC, Standard Chartered, Bandhan MF, and Kotak, Signature Global is known for its disciplined approach, quality execution, and adherence to global standards. With strategically located projects across South of Gurugram, SPR, Dwarka Expressway, and New Gurugram, the brand has set benchmarks in timely delivery and value creation.
